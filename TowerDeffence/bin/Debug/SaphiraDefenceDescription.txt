Saphira Defence is a game of type Tower Defence and it's created by Saphira OOP Team. 
The goal of tower defense games is to try to stop enemies from crossing a map by building traps to 
slow them down and towers which shoot at them as they pass. Enemies and towers usually have varied abilities, 
costs, and upgrade prices. When an enemy is defeated, the player earns money or points, 
which are used to buy or upgrade towers, or upgrade the number of money or points that are earned,
or even upgrade the rate at which they upgrade.
